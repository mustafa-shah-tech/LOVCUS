Payment & Policy

1. Payment Policy

We work on a customized basis. Therefore, a minimum 30% advance payment via Bank Transfer, JazzCash, or Easypaisa is mandatory. The remaining amount can be paid on delivery.

2. Delivery

We ship nationwide in Pakistan. Delivery time is 3-5 working days after order confirmation.

3. Refunds & Returns

Since items are customized with names or special dates, we do not offer refunds unless the item arrives damaged or broken.
